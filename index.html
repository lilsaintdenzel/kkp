KKP Online Children's Bible SchoolProgram Overview

At KKP Online Bible School, our mission is to help children grow spiritually, intellectually, and in character as Kings and Priests, through fun, interactive, and age-appropriate lessons that teach Godly principles for daily life. Parents are key partners in this journey, supporting participation and reinforcing learning at home.

How the School Runs
Flexible Duration & Scheduling
  • Parents can choose a year-long program or shorter semesters. Each enrollment runs for a minimum of 3 months.
  • Live online classes are held once or twice weekly, depending on parent preference. Classes are recorded for review if children miss a session.
  • Class times can be fixed based on parents' availability, making the program flexible for family schedules.

Age Groups, Parent Involvement& Class Structure

Age Groups & Group Sessions

Parents may choose group sessions for children with peers:

Ages 5–8

Royal Seeds

🌱 First steps in serving God and learning His Word

Children begin their journey, discovering Godly principles in a fun and age-appropriate way.

Ages 9–12

Kingdom Ambassadors

⚔️ Building faith, character, and skills

These children are learning to represent Christ in their daily lives and preparing for greater responsibility in God's kingdom.

Ages 13–18

Kingdom Leaders

👑 Stepping into spiritual authority and leadership

Teens develop deeper knowledge of God's Word, character, and leadership skills to impact their generation.

  • Parents are encouraged to actively participate in their child's learning journey, reinforcing lessons at home and helping with assignments when needed.
  • Parents receive regular updates on progress, assignments, and spiritual growth.

Meet the Lead Tutor

Precious Ajanaku is a devoted child of God with a deep passion for raising children who know, love, and walk with Him from an early age. She believes that children are full participants in God's Kingdom and should never be left out of what the Kingdom is truly about.

Her mission is to help children understand their identity, authority, and purpose as Kings and Priests in Christ, equipping them to grow in faith, character, and leadership from a young age.

Background & Expertise

A trained medical professional, Precious brings discipline, care, and responsibility into her teaching and mentoring. She has years of experience nurturing children in God's Word as a children's Sunday school teacher at her family church.

Ministries She Leads
📖 KKP Online Bible School 🌿 JourneyThroughTheWord with TLA 👑 The Daughters Evolving Ministry
